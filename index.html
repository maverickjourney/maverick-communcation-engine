What Maverick Journey AI Does

Middle-man AI
Collects: client messages, food logs, photos, injuries, documents, reminders, questions.
Organizes: client profile, dashboard, nutrition log, photo library, reminder system, weekly report.
Summarizes: what changed, who needs attention, what the trainer should review.
Flags: missed check-ins, soreness, low sleep, low water, possible retention risk.
Drafts: reminders, follow-ups, trainer messages, weekly summaries.

Important Boundaries

Safety
Bloodwork and health documentsMaverick Journey can organize and summarize file context, but should not diagnose, prescribe, or replace medical review.
No diagnosis
Injury notesAI flags the trainer. The trainer decides whether to adjust workouts, call, or refer out.
Flag only
Trainer remains in controlAI drafts and organizes. Trainer approves messages and coaching decisions.

How to explain this to trainers

Presentation script

“Slack is not the main dashboard. Slack is the communication layer.”

Your clients already text you everything: meals, soreness, weight, photos, schedule issues, injuries, questions, and random updates. Maverick Journey brings that into Slack so the AI can organize it automatically.

Each client gets their own Slack space. They can send anything they want the trainer to know. Maverick Journey collects it, sorts it, and updates the trainer dashboard. Then the trainer can send reminders, feedback, workout updates, or decide when to call/text directly.

For the Client

They get an easy place to send everything without learning a complicated fitness app.

For the Trainer

They get organized communication across every client instead of scattered texts and forgotten updates.

For the AI

Maverick Journey becomes the middle layer that turns conversations into organized coaching intelligence.
